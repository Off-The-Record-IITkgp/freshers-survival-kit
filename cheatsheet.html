Online Payments for Under-18s

If you're under 18 and don’t have a debit card, try Fampay or Akudo. You’ll need:

  • Parent’s consent (KYC required)
  • Your phone number and basic details
  • Once verified, use UPI, card, or scan QR at shops

Useful for: Mess recharge, Zomato, Swiggy, Amazon, Paytm, etc.
