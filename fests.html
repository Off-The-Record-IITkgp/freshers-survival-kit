🌸 Spring Fest (SF)

KGP’s flagship cultural fest held in January. Includes concerts, dance, dramatics, fashion shows, stand-up comedy, and more. Expect crowds, food stalls, and pro-nights with celebrities!

⚙️ Kshitij (KTJ)

Asia’s largest techno-management fest. January–February. Features robotics, design, coding, AI, and management competitions. Also hosts industry talks and exhibitions.

  • Infest (Innovation Cell)
  • Spark (Entrepreneurship Cell)
  • Galaxy, Literati, Jam, etc. (From clubs like Debsoc, Dramsoc, DC++)

🧑‍🎓 Freshers & Farewells

Informal fests for bonding and celebration. Usually organized hall-wise or department-wise. Most are costume-themed and have food, games, and performances.

🔔 How to Stay Updated

  • Join your batch’s Telegram/WhatsApp groups
  • Follow Gymkhana & Hall Instagrams
  • Check notice boards near Tikka, Cheddi, or library
  • Club pages and word-of-mouth are key
