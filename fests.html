🎉 KGP Fests & Festivals

⚙️ Techno Fest +

Kshitij (KTJ): Asia’s largest techno-management fest, held in January. Features robotics, design, coding, AI, management competitions, industry talks, and exhibitions.

🎭 Cultural Fest +

Spring Fest (SF): KGP’s flagship cultural festival, usually in January. Includes concerts, dance, dramatics, fashion shows, stand-up comedy, pro-nights, and a buzzing food scene.

🏆 Sports Fest +

Shaurya: The inter-college sports fest with competitions in athletics, football, cricket, and other major sports, bringing together participants from across India.

🪔 Festivals +

  • Illumination (Diwali): Iconic DIY diya and candle lighting structures, a hallmark of KGP tradition.
  • Holi: Colorful celebrations across halls and campus grounds.

📚 Department Fests +

  • ChemClave (Chemical Engineering)
  • Mechafest (Mechanical Engineering)
  • Composit (Aerospace)
  • Infest (Innovation Cell)
  • Spark (Entrepreneurship Cell)
  • Galaxy, Literati, JAM, etc. (DebSoc, Dramsoc, DC++)

🌏 Regional Fests +

  • North East Fest (Celebrating culture of the NE states)
  • Tamil Fest (By Tamil Sangam)
  • Rajasthani Fest (By Rajasthani Students’ Association)
  • Telugu Fest (By Telugu Cultural Group)
  • And many more regional celebrations organized by student associations.

🔔 How to Stay Updated +

  • Join your batch’s Telegram/WhatsApp groups
  • Follow Gymkhana & Hall Instagram pages
  • Check for posters in halls
  • Word-of-mouth & club pages are the fastest way to get updates
