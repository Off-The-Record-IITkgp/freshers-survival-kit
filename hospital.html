Medical Help

  • Campus Hospital (BCRH): Open till 5PM, take IIT ID, OPD file at counter.
  • After Hours: Contact BCRH emergency room or Security Section.
  • When to go home: If you have high fever, severe infections, or long rest needed.
  • See seniors when: You feel confused, need help navigating system or are scared.
  • Health docs to keep: OPD slips, test results, prior prescriptions.
