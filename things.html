College Essentials (Buy at Tech Market) ✦

  • Transport: Cycle
  • Academic: FX-991 ES Plus Scientific Calculator
  • Stationery:
    • 5-subject notebook / multiple notebooks
    • Pens & pencils
    • Eraser & sharpener
    • Stapler & pins
    • Gum / glue
    • Whitener
  • Hostel Basics: Bucket, mug, water bottle, mattress, pillow, bedsheets (thin for summer + thick blankets for winter), towels, curtains (not needed for girl freshers — provided in halls), wall hooks
  • Toiletries:
    • Toothbrush & toothpaste
    • Soap & shampoo
    • Detergent (liquid/powder)
    • Razor
    • Comb
  • Clothing & Seasonal: Slippers, shoes (mandatory for labs & fitness), a pair of formal wear (shirt, trouser, shoes), raincoat, umbrella, sweaters, hoodies, caps, arm coverage sleeves (if needed)
  • Gadgets: Earphones/headphones, laptop (can be skipped until 2nd year if on budget), phone, extension box
  • Other Must-Haves: Knife, scissors, medicines (fever, cough/cold, allergy meds, ORS), cleaning wipes, dustbin, laundry basket, kettle, laptop bag/waterproof bag

Things to Bring from Home ✦

  • Documents: Aadhar card, college allotment letter, JEE admit card, and other official documents
  • Food: Pickles & snacks from home

Optional Comfort Items ✦

  • Study lamp
  • Organizer
  • Cooler/fan for hot summers
  • Calendar
  • Speaker
  • Idol of God (if religious)

Things to Borrow (Secondhand from Seniors) ✦

  • Lab coat for chemistry lab (used for only 1 semester)
  • Engineering drawing kit – drafter, tool box. (Not used again except for architecture, mechanical, civil and chemical departments.)

Things to Buy Secondhand ✦

  • Reference books
